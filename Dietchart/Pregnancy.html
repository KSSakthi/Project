Pregnancy Nutrition Chart

Sample Diet Chart

This sample pregnancy nutrition chart provides a general idea of a balanced diet for pregnant individuals. It's essential to consult with a healthcare provider or a registered dietitian for a personalized nutrition plan tailored to your specific needs during pregnancy.

During pregnancy, it's important to focus on getting a variety of nutrients, including folic acid, iron, calcium, and protein. Ensure that you maintain proper hydration and follow any dietary recommendations provided by your healthcare team.
